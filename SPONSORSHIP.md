# Sponsorship

Thank you for considering sponsoring the **OWASP DockSec** project!

DockSec is an open-source project maintained by the community. Your support helps us:

- Maintain and improve the AI-powered scanning engine.
- Cover costs for CI/CD and infrastructure.
- Support community events and outreach.

## Ways to Sponsor

### GitHub Sponsors
You can sponsor us directly through GitHub: [Sponsor OWASP DockSec](https://github.com/sponsors/OWASP)

### OWASP Foundation
As an official OWASP project, you can also support us through the OWASP Foundation: [Support OWASP](https://owasp.org/donate/)

### Corporate Sponsorship
If your organization uses DockSec and would like to support its development, please reach out to the project leaders.
